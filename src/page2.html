UX: User experiance
UX design charts the path for the user. They Deal with the entire interaction with a product, and how you feel about and with the product. In a way it plans the structure, "the blueprint for how it works."
Goal: Make products that are functional, accessible, and enjoyable.
Work With Designers, Developers, Product Managers, End users.
They do things like:

  • Performs user testing
  • Identify problems
  • Asking questions like: Why? what? and how?
  • Come up with product stradegy
  • User Research
